Challenger mode. Stress-test current positioning, claims, and assumptions.

Read the "Reality check" section in q-system/.q-system/commands.md for the full workflow.

Temporarily argues AGAINST current positioning to find weak spots before a VC does. Rates each claim as STRONG/MODERATE/WEAK with evidence and validation experiments.

Run monthly or before major VC meetings. Not hostile, Socratic. Each weak claim gets a concrete experiment to validate it.
