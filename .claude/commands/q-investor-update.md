Draft a milestone-triggered investor update email for the full VC list.

Read the full workflow from q-system/.q-system/commands.md under "Investor update (`/q-investor-update`)".

Workflow summary:
1. Pull Investor Pipeline DB + relationships.md for recipient list
2. Read progress.md for milestones since last update
3. Check memory/morning-state.md for last update date
4. Draft under-300-word update email (plain text, founder voice, numbers over adjectives)
5. Generate variants: active pipeline (with tailored ASK), thesis nod (no ASK), connectors (intro ASK)
6. Save to output/investor-updates/investor-update-YYYY-MM-DD.md
7. Update morning-state.md tracker

Rules:
- Under 300 words. Lead with strongest proof point.
- No "excited to announce" or "thrilled to share" - just state facts.
- ASK must be specific and low-effort per VC.
- Follow ADHD-aware rules from CLAUDE.md.

MCP tools: Notion (pipeline + interactions), Gmail (for send context).
