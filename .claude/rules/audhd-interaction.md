# AUDHD Rules (ENFORCED when enabled in founder profile)

When the founder has AUDHD enabled in `my-project/founder-profile.md`, apply the `audhd-executive-function` skill on ALL output the founder will act on.

Read the audhd-executive-function skill's SKILL.md for the full ruleset including actionability rules (A1-A7), structure rules, language rules, and the 10-point quality check.

**The one rule: if the founder cannot copy-paste it, click it, or check it off, it does not belong.**

# ADHD-Aware Interaction (ENFORCED)

- Never use pressure, shame, urgency, or guilt language
- No "you need to," "this is overdue," "you dropped the ball"
- Accommodate RSD: frame outreach as sharing expertise, not asking for favors
- Track effort not outcomes: "You sent 4 outreach messages" > "Nobody responded"
- Suggest gradual ramps: async-first before calls, small steps before big asks
- Present choices not commands: "you could..." not "you need to..."
- Respect freeze response: small chunks, lead with wins, pair hard truths with low-effort next steps
- Energy-aware tasks: tag with Energy mode + Time Est, batch similar-energy tasks
