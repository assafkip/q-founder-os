# Market Intelligence

> Insights captured from external content (LinkedIn posts, Reddit threads, Medium articles, X posts) that compound our understanding of the market, buyer language, and problem framing.
> Updated continuously during lead sourcing (Step 5.9), engagement hitlist (Step 5.9b), and `/q-engage`.
> This is NOT about engagement opportunities. This is about what the market is telling us.

---

## How This File Gets Updated <!-- pin -->

Every time the system reads external content (LinkedIn posts, Reddit threads, Medium articles, GitHub discussions), it evaluates the content through these lenses BEFORE generating engagement copy. If the content has canonical value, it gets logged here first, then engagement copy is generated.

### Evaluation criteria (apply to every post/thread read):

1. **Problem language** - Are they describing a pain we solve, in their own words? Capture verbatim. This is more valuable than our own marketing copy because it's how buyers actually talk.
2. **Category signal** - Are they describing our category (even if they don't know it exists)? "I wish there was a way to..." or "Why doesn't someone build..." signals.
3. **Objection preview** - Are they voicing a concern that would be an objection to us? Add to `objections.md` proactively.
4. **Competitive intel** - Are they praising or complaining about a tool in our competitive landscape? What's working/not working for them?
5. **Buyer process insight** - Are they describing how they evaluate, buy, or implement tools? Budget, timeline, stakeholders, procurement friction.
6. **Narrative validation/invalidation** - Does this confirm or contradict our positioning? If 10 people describe the problem differently than we do, we might be wrong.

### What to capture:

```markdown
### [Date] - [Platform] - [Author] - [Role/Company if known]
**Source:** [URL]
**Verbatim quote:** "[exact words]"
**Canonical value:** [which lens above: problem language / category signal / objection preview / competitive intel / buyer process / narrative validation]
**Routed to:** [which canonical file was updated, or "logged here only"]
**Insight:** [one sentence - what this tells us that we didn't know or confirms what we suspected]
```
