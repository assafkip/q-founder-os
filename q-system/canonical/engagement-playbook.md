# Social Engagement Playbook

> Rules for commenting, DMs, and relationship building on social platforms.

## Core Rules <!-- pin -->

1. **Never pitch your product in comments** unless directly asked
2. **Max 3-4 sentences** per comment
3. **1 comment per person per week** (unless they reply)
4. **Every engagement logged** to tracker (Notion or local file)
5. **Comment style adapted to relationship type:**
   - Investors: domain insight, market perspective
   - Practitioners: peer validation, shared experience
   - Connectors: amplification, bridge-building

## Comment Styles

### Insight Style
Add a non-obvious observation. Connect their post to something they haven't considered. Show you think about this deeply.

### Connector Style
Bridge their idea to someone else's work or a related trend. "This connects to what [person] was saying about..."

### Question Style
Ask a genuine question that advances the conversation. Not "great post!" but a question that shows you engaged with the content.

## DM Rules <!-- pin -->

- Start with "I" not their name (never "Hey Sarah, been thinking...")
- One ask OR one question. Never both.
- No "hope you're well" or "hope this finds you"
- Get to the point in 2-3 sentences
- Sign off with just your first name (no Best/Cheers/Regards)

## Engagement Tracking

Log every engagement with:
- Person name
- Platform
- Type (comment / DM / connection request)
- Content summary
- Date
- Follow-up date (7 days out)
