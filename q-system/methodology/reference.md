## Rules for Responsible Online Research

When using web access for research:
1. **Pattern validation only** - confirm that market signals, competitor claims, or buyer behaviors match what you've heard in conversations
2. **No trend chasing** - don't adopt positioning because "the market is moving toward X"
3. **No citations required** - but reasoning must reflect real-world signals, not speculation
4. **Competitive intelligence** - check substitute bucket positioning, pricing signals, and analyst coverage
5. **Never copy competitor language** - understand their positioning to differentiate, not to imitate
