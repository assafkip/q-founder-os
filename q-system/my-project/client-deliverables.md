# Client Deliverables

Active client commitments tracked by the morning pipeline (06-client-deliverables agent).

## Format

| Client | Deliverable | Due Date | Status |
|--------|------------|----------|--------|
| {{CLIENT_NAME}} | {{DELIVERABLE}} | {{DUE_DATE}} | in-progress |

## Status Values

- `in-progress` - actively being worked on
- `blocked` - waiting on something
- `delivered` - completed and sent
- `overdue` - past due date, not delivered
