# Lead Lifecycle Rules

> When to advance, park, re-engage, or kill a lead.

## Statuses

| Status | Definition | Entry criteria | Exit criteria |
|--------|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| **New** | Just identified | ICP match | First outreach sent |
| **Contacted** | First outreach sent | DM/email sent | Reply received OR 14 days no response |
| **Warm** | Engaged in conversation | Replied to outreach | Meeting booked OR conversation stalled |
| **Hot** | Active opportunity | Meeting booked or demo requested | Deal closed OR went cold |
| **Active** | Ongoing relationship | Regular interaction | Relationship matures or fades |
| **Cooling** | Engagement dropping | No interaction in 14 days | Re-engage or park |
| **Parked** | Not now, maybe later | Explicit "not now" or 30 days cold | Re-engage trigger fires |
| **Dead** | Not a fit | Confirmed not ICP or explicit no | — |

## Auto-Rules (enforced in morning routine)

- **14 days no response after outreach** → Move to Cooling, suggest one follow-up
- **30 days no interaction** → Move to Parked (NOT Dead — people come back)
- **2+ failed follow-ups** → Move to Parked with reason
- **Explicit "not interested"** → Move to Dead, log reason
- **Reply after being Parked** → Auto-reactivate to Warm

## Re-engagement Triggers

- They post about a problem you solve
- They change jobs/roles
- They engage with your content
- Market event makes your solution more relevant
- 90 days since parking (quarterly check)

## Kill Rules

Never kill a lead just because they haven't responded. Kill only when:
1. Confirmed not ICP (wrong company size, wrong industry, wrong role)
2. Explicit "not interested, don't contact again"
3. Company shut down or person left the industry
