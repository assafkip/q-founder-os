Navigate to https://www.linkedin.com/analytics/post-summary/

For each of the 10 most recent posts, extract:
- **post_url**: Direct URL to the post
- **text**: First 100 characters of the post text
- **impressions**: Number of impressions
- **likes**: Number of likes
- **comments**: Number of comments
- **reposts**: Number of reposts

Stop after 10 posts.
